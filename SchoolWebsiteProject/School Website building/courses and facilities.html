Courses and facilities

Amlajorah High School is a Bengali-medium coeducational institution established in 1947. It has facilities for teaching from class V to class XII.

Library

School owns a library for students and staff and there are near about 518 books in the library.

Computer Lab

There is a computer lab in school for learning purpose and almost 18 computers are there in the computer lab To make teaching learning process joyful, interesting and easy to understand through audio-visual aids School has implemented CAL (Computer Aided Learning) or better known as Computer Based Learning. CAL is currently functional in the school. .
